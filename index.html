Research Interests

I am an associate professor at the Institute of Psychology,
Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznan.

My research interests include the following thematic areas:

(1) Determinants and correlates of singlehood
(2) The link between relationship status and mental health
(3) Psychology of intimate relationships
(4) Cultural adaptation of foreign measurement tools
(5) Somatopsychic dependencies in patients treated conservatively due to adolescent idiopathic scoliosis
(6) Human development in young adulthood
(7) Psychology of sex and gender
